Battleship

How to Play

  • Goal: Sink all 5 enemy ships before the AI sinks yours.
  • Placement: Select a ship, choose orientation using the Horizontal/Vertical button (or left‑click = horizontal, right‑click = vertical on desktop), then tap or click your grid. Use double‑click or press R to randomize all ships, or tap/click a placed ship to reposition it.
  • Battle: Tap or click enemy grid cells to fire. Hits = 🔥, Misses = •, Sunk = 🔥marker.
  • Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2).
  • Tip: Press R to randomize ship placement at any time during the placement phase.
